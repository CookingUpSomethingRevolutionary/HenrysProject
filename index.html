About Me

Hi! I'm Henry, and I'm 13 years old. At the time of writing, I'll be turning 14 in just 11 days! Here are some of my favorite things:

Favorite Movie: The Electric State

Set in an alternate 1990s where humanity has survived a devastating war against intelligent robots, The Electric State follows Michelle, a teenage orphan, as she travels across the abandoned American West with a mysterious robot searching for her missing brother. Along the way, she teams up with a smuggler and uncovers secrets that could change everything she thought she knew about the war, the robots, and her own family.

Favorite TV Show: The Amazing Digital Circus

After putting on a mysterious virtual reality headset, six strangers find themselves trapped inside a bizarre digital world controlled by the unpredictable AI ringmaster, Caine. Unable to escape, they're forced to survive increasingly strange adventures while trying to hold onto their sanity and memories. Beneath the colorful animation and comedy lies an unsettling mystery about the circus, its creator, and whether the characters will ever find a way home.

Favorite Video Game: Trombone Champ

Unlike most rhythm games, Trombone Champ gives players complete control of a virtual trombone, letting them slide between notes while playing everything from classical music to internet memes. Its intentionally chaotic gameplay, over-the-top humor, and surprisingly addictive mechanics make every performance feel unique—even when it's hilariously off-key.
